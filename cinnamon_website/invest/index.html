The problem

Today, 8 out of 9 causes of death are preventable. All the big killers - coronary heart disease, stroke, diabetes, cancer, Alzheimer - share an early clinical presentation, called Metabolic Syndrome.
High blood sugar is in most cases what leads to this cascade of events, by causing diabetes or pre-diabetes.

382 millions worldwide have diabetes, 46% of which do not even know it; by 2035, this number will spike to 592 millions, a 55% increase. The cause of this sugar epidemic is in our diet: we develop a sugar addiction because 80% of the food we buy at the grocery store hides some sort of sugar. Even bread, salad dressing and fish sticks!

The solution

Cinnamon breaks the sugar addiction cycle, and makes it possible to prevent or revert Metabolic Syndrome.

It helps you keep your blood sugar checked at all times and motivates you to remain within a safe and steady range.
Through continuous feedback, personalised tips and a unique gardening interface, it also helps you achieve a diet mostly based on plants and real, healthy food.

Roadmap

What we are building:

  • Glucose tracking tool
  • Software-based personalized feedback and tips
  • Plant based diet program

We are focusing on a specific set of users who already have a big need to solve this problem and have no ideal solution yet: Diabetics type 1 and 2.
As simple sugar tracking through body fluids develops and becomes readily available, we will target the general population.
