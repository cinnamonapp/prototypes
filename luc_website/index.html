LYN IS HERE!

A little friend - an effortless way to track your glucose - a healthy life.

Meet LYN

A CARING GLUCOSE BUDDY

LYN allows you to better capture blood glucose stats either for yourself or your loved ones. Every time you capture a reading, Lyn eats, grows and gets to know you. Take care of LYN and learn more about yourself thanks to several insights on your health, patterns on your daily habits and plenty of games + fun activities.

LYN is a reflection of your committment to care for what matters. Because healthcare should be personal, joyful and inspired by people.




WHAT MAKES LYN SPECIAL?

(and super cute)


GLUCOSE LOG

Every time you tap on LYN's mouth, you can feed it by adding the value you just measured with you blood glucose meter. Watch LYN enjoy its meal and inform you on how adherent and thorough you've been in capturing your readings.

INSIGHTS ENGINE

LYN remembers when and where you capture the information. After a few days LYN starts nudging you, supporting you and acknowledging your patterns. In a few weeks' time you'll be inseparable.

HEALTHY GAMES

Every action counts. Especially if you want to see LYN change color or shape and start to personalize it. Whenever you hit a good average or sustain your tracking behavior, LYN presents you with micro challenges that can unlock nice little surprises.

MEASURE

Synchronize your blood glucose meter or manually insert the readings in order to get a better picture of health.
LYN will generate for you a clear list of readings & beautiful graphs, all chronologically sorted.
Bring LYN with you at your doctor's appointments or share easily your progress over email.
